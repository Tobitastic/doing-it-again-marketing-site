  • Spanning 13 episodes over 3 volumes this is a detailed exploration of trans women's relationship and hookup dynamics, This film series weaves together explicit scenes and interview footage from a diverse range trans women and their partners to paint a holistic portrait about trans women's sexuality. Award winning director Tobi Hill-Meyer picks up where she left off with her debut film, Doing it Ourselves
